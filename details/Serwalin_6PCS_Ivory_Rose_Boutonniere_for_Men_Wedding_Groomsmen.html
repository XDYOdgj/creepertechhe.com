Elegant & Timeless Wedding Boutonnieres

Serwalin's exquisite ivory rose boutonnieres are a blend of elegance and realism, crafted with care for every detail. Each piece features hand-painted white silk roses paired with lifelike plastic eucalyptus leaves, offering a sophisticated look without the need for fresh flowers. The vintage twine adds a charming touch, ensuring a balanced and stylish presentation that complements any formal attire.

Perfect for weddings, proms, anniversaries, and formal events, these 6PCS boutonnieres are designed to make a lasting impression. Whether you're the groom, best man, or part of the groomsmen, they provide a versatile and durable accessory. Choose between the secure metal clip or the strong pin for easy attachment and confidence during your event.

  • Handmade with Realistic Silk Roses: Each rose is carefully crafted to look like a true blossom, offering a fresh yet low-maintenance alternative.

  • Vintage Twine Design: This elegant element adds a nostalgic charm, maintaining a classic and refined style.
  • Two Wearing Options: The metal clip ensures a secure fit, while the pin offers added stability for dancing or movement.
  • Perfect for Occasions: Ideal for weddings, proms, formal dinners, and other special events where style and sophistication are key.
